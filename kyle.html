About Kyle

My name is Kyle Diaz and I am currently studying IT at SAIT. I initially got into IT because of my favourite activities, gaming and PC building.

My favourite game would have to be Red Dead Redemption II. The open-world is vast and the narrative is incredible. I'm starting my second playthrough and my goal is to 100% the game during the end of semester break.



I started the second playthrough because I got a great deal on an OLED monitor at Memory Express, my first playthrough was done on a 1080p LCD TV, so playing on a 4K OLED display has breathed whole new life into the experience for me.
