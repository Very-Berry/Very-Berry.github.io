Ethan's Drone Photography

Hi everyone! My name is Ethan. I'm 21, born and raised in Calgary, and I love any and all kinds of technology. I build computers, fix phones, and, you guessed it, I fly drones! Well, I fly A drone. It's a DJI Mavic Mini that I purchased a few years ago, which has a fairly decent camera with a 3-axis gimbal, GPS functionality, and a controller that streams a live-feed to my phone. Here's a video that I took of the downtown skyline a while ago! Its also a lot of fun (and a lot more difficult) taking action videos, like these two I took of my wonderful girlfriend wakesurfing! To make things even more difficult, I'm actually piloting the drone while sitting on the boat you see here! I'm not very experienced at video editing, so these are fairly long clips and they may not be interesting enough to watch the whole thing. I've also tried to reduce the resolution and compress them so that they will hopefully stream consistently over the internet, so the quality may not look great, but the original footage is 2.7k resolution at 30fps! I apologize if the videos don't work at all.

About the Drone!

Here are some pictures of the drone itself. As you can see, it folds up, is very compact, and can easily fit inside even a small backpack with its case. Without the case, it will even fit inside a large pocket or fanny pack! Unfolded, the drone is only about 30cm at it's widest point. It also only weighs 249 grams, which means that in most countries there are very few rules that affect where I can (legally) fly. I don't even have to register the drone, or get any kind of license! I do need to be responsible though, and always be mindful of my surroundings. In case anyone's interested in the technical specs, here they are.

  • Weight: 249g
  • Max Speed: 13m/s (46.8km/h)
  • Max Flight Time: 30 minutes
  • Sensor Resolution: 12MP
  • Max Video Resolution: 2720x1530 30fps
  • Max Video Bitrate: 40mbps
